Send a tweets to the Moon

Moontweet is collecting 10.000.000 messages in form of short tweets to be delivered to the Moon by the aerospace company Astrobotic in late 2017. The Tweets will be stored in digital format within a capsule integrated into a single Moon Pod and stay on the lunar surface for century to come.
